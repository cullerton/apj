Acoustic Pizza Jam

What Pizza and Friends
When 6pm, on the 15th of the month
Where Mike and Zoey's
Bring Pizza toppings, desserts, munchies, acoustic instruments, fun toys, etc.

When my friend Leanne moved into the neighborhood, she brought a wonderful tradition of gathering once a month for community dinner. Leanne usually cooked the main course, and everyone else brought what they could.

Sometimes it was a packed house, with three generations of friends and family eating together. Other times, there were more dogs than humans. But it was always a great time to gather with friends, eat good food, and relax--even when the dogs were barking and the kids were crying.

This is my spin on her practice, while she is away.

Please bring your favorite pizza topping, or something else to share. We will provide all the fixings for a delicious homemade cheese pizza. The rest is up to you. That means the toppings you bring determine the pizzas we eat. So make sure it's something I like!

This is a family friendly event. Children passing the dog test(1) are welcome.

The point is to spend time with each other.

(1) Children must pass the dog test. This means they must be as well-behaved as the dogs. This may sound like an easy test. It is not.
